About Me

Jenna M. graduated from UCLA with College Honors and Magna Cum Laude. As a freshman, Jenna received perfect essay scores in several English classes; professors used her essays as examples to teach the rest of the class. Subsequently, Jenna was chosen to work at the UCLA Undergraduate Writing Center to tutor and assist undergraduate and graduate students with their writing needs, including graduate school and job applications (personal statements, resumes, & cover letters), research papers (in APA, MLA, or Chicago Style formats), analytical papers, and other class assignments.


Jenna has worked with students of many ages, ranging from elementary school to graduate school, both in-person and online. Her students have moved on to study at some of the nation’s top universities, including Stanford, Harvard, Yale, UCLA, UC Berkeley, and USC. Since graduating, Jenna has taught English to young students in Beijing. She’s also tutored in other subjects via several top-tier teaching platforms. Ultimately, Jenna fosters her students to become self-sufficient writers and editors.


Beyond private tutoring, Jenna plans to transform the education landscape so that students are encouraged to take risks, make mistakes, collaborate, and discover.
